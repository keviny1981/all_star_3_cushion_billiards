PRIVACY POLICY

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we ("us," or "our") collect, use, and share information from users of our mobile and desktop game, "All Star 3 Cushion Billiards" (the "Game").

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy aims to be transparent about what data is collected and for what purpose.

2. Information We Collect

Since "All Star 3 Cushion Billiards" is an offline-focused game with no account system, we collect very limited information directly from you.

Information You Provide:

  • Nickname: Provided for display purposes in multiplayer matches. It is not unique and does not store personal information.

Information Collected by Third-Party Services:

  • Multiplayer Services (Epic Games): The Game's multiplayer feature uses Epic Games Online Services (EOSG) for peer-to-peer matching. When you connect, EOSG may collect data related to your connection, such as your IP address, to facilitate the match. This data is subject to the Epic Games Privacy Policy, which you can review here: https://legal.epicgames.com/en-US/epicgames/privacy-policy.
  • Advertising (Admob): The Android version of the Game uses Google's Admob service to display advertisements. Admob automatically collects certain data from your device to provide relevant ads. This data may include, but is not limited to, your IP address, device identifiers (like the Android Advertising ID), and usage data.
  • App Management (Google Firebase): We use Firebase to manage application stability and features. This service may collect installation identifiers and device information to facilitate "Force Updates" and manage feature toggles (such as turning advertisements on or off).

The collection and use of data by Google services (Admob and Firebase) are governed by the Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Enable and facilitate multiplayer matches.
  • Display advertisements and manage app features (via Firebase Remote Config).
  • Ensure users are running the latest version of the Game for security and compatibility.

4. How We Share Information

We do not share any information you provide directly to us with third parties.

Any data collected by Admob and Epic Games (EOSG) is shared with and used by those companies in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the limited information we collect from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will post the new policy on our website and update the "Last Updated" date at the top. Your continued use of the Game after any such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
